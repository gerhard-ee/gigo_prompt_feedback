How to Craft Better Prompts - Get Feedback!

So, how can you get better at writing prompts? Practice, and get feedback! Here are some tips:

1. Define Your Goal: What do you want the AI to do? (Summarize, explain, create, compare?)
2. Be Specific: Instead of "Tell me about dogs," try "What are three common behavioral issues in Labrador Retrievers and how can they be managed?"
3. Provide Context: If asking for a story, specify genre, tone, characters.
4. Use Keywords: Help the AI narrow its search and understanding.
5. Iterate & Refine: Don't expect perfection on the first try. Analyze the output and tweak your prompt.
